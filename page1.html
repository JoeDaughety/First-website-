My first dirty heads show!

The dirty heads were founded in 2006 and i like to belive ive been a fan since day one. In 2008 they were really hitting the ground running and doing any show they could get... Which lead them to playing at the opening of a Cali taco chain that was making the debut in HTX! It was an accoustic set, and they only played infront of about 25 people, but its one of those shows that meant so much to me back then, and really set the tone for a music genere that would change my life forever.

First trip to Caliroots

My First trip to cali roots was one of the best trips of my life. I was probably around 21 and it was the first time i took a trip on my own with some friends. We were broke and coulent afford a plane ticket and hotel room so me and two of my best friends packed up my truck with food, drinks, and our camping gear and left for caliroots late on a Tuesday evening. We were leaving from Houston Texas and were looking at about a 24 hour trip to get to Los Angeles... our first pit stop to get some rest before the show. The three of us took turns driving and switched every 4-5 hours, we were all young and ready to get there so we made really great time and made it to LA in just about 24 hours. We had some friends that lived in a one bedroom apartment and the 3 of us crashed in their living room and got some rest. Waking up Thursday morning we were all stoked an ready to get back on the road. We were about 4 hours from the monteray fair grounds. Getting there was easy, but once we got to the fair grounds we could hardly contain the excitment! We were camping so we got to work on building out our area for the next three days... Our three man tent was all we had and we couldnt have been happier. The next three days were full of some of the best days of music any of us had experienced up to taht point in our lives. We made friends with strangers and loved every waking moment of it. Alot of the details are a bit of a blur... but id give anything to go back in time and relive our first trip to cali roots!

Red Rocks

My first show at Red Rocks... Man was it special. Ive wanted to go to Red Rocks for as long as I can remember, and two months after my 30th birthday I finally had the change. My girlfriend Aly and I were planning a vacation in Key stone Colorado, and everything lined up perfectly. We landed in Denver on Saturday and our show at RR was that Sunday. The stars of the show that night were hands down Rebelution, but the moment ill remember for the rest of my life is watching the Bassist of Steel Pulse RUN through the crowd from the first row to the very last and taking with picutes with everyone he could along the way. It was so awesome to see somone that loved what he did as much as Amkak Tafari did that night. I was lucky enough to snag a picute with him, its a little blury, but I wouldnt change a thing.
